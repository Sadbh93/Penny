Penny

Penny is a typical teenager, trying to carve out independence away from her embarassing parents. She is a black feminist, very logical and often bratty. She's also a talented singer, footballer, journalist and straight A student.

Her Friends

Unfortunately she often fails to live up to her potential, and spends most of her time in the company of other teenagers with whom she has quite toxic relationships. Her so called friends frequently lead her into dangerous situations and abandon her.

Her Family

Although embrassed by them, Penny generally has a good relationship with her family. They live three generations together, and Penny is a second mother to her baby twin brother and sister. She regularly gets caught between her conservative father and wildcard grandmother who have opposing ideas on how much freedom a teenager should have
